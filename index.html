Scoop's Guide to Murky

There's no wrong way to play Murky :)

Following is a guide on how to start playing Murky as a beginner in Heroes of the Storm!

We will go through talents, laning phase, and teamfighting.

Talent Choices

  • Level 1

    Level 1 we will almost exclusively take A Fishy Deal for the Mercenary camp bribes

  • Level 4

    Here we will go with Tufferfish to allow for easier waveclear. Once you become more comfortable, I'd suggeset taking Slime Time

  • Level 7

    Slippery When Wet is almost a must have for the 2 second reduction on Safety Bubble

  • Level 10

    More often than not we will take Octograb for the pick potential prior to teamfights.

  • Level 13

    Fish Tank is a must have! With no bribe stacks, this allows you to easily solo mercenary camps

  • Level 16

    Fish Oil allows for one-button waveclear, along with burst damage against enemy Heroes!

  • Level 20

    Making Inky is flat out oppressive. 2 second cd on Slime is a great way to deal constant damage, as well as constantly heal yourself in teamfights

Laning Phase

  • Place that Egg!

    Don't forget to place your egg!

    Unlike normal heroes, murky has a SUPER short respawn time, and his deaths only count toward 25% of a kill!

    Once dead, murky will take about 6 seconds to hatch from his egg, so be sure to place it in a safe spot close to your lane!

  • Kill those minions!

    utilizing the pufferfish to get killing blows on minions is essential for getting bribe stacks!

    Try to utilize the Q > wait 1 second > W > then Q. With this, the minions should be weakened enough to take the killing blow from pufferfish.

    Note that once you are level 4, one Q and one W will kill the entire wave of minions

  • Bribe those Mercs!

    This is a mercenary camp. If you can defeat them, they will become temporary allies and help destroy enemy towers!

    With the level 1 talent 'A Fishy Deal', we can bribe these mercenaries to join us without having to take the time to defeat them

    Murky can be especially useful for bribing mercs on the enemy side of the map

Teamfighting as Murky

  • Get to the objective ahead of time to drop your egg in a sneaky spot!

    It's very important to ensure it's hidden so the enemy team doesn't destroy it

    Good egg placement can help get you back into the fight quickly

  • Slime(Q) an enemy and quickly drop the pufferfish(W)

    Follow this up with an Octograb(R)

    having a few teammates around to assist in finishing off the kill is essential! Try not to do this alone!

  • Use your Slime(Q) and Safety Bubble(E) to be as annoying as possible during the teamfight

    Using the slow from your slime and big area of the pufferfish can be useful to help teammates chase down or escape from enemies

    If the teamfight lasts long enough, you may be able to Octograb(R) again since it has a very short cooldown!

    Look to finish off enemies, and keep them off the objective. It's okay to die a few times as Murky if you have your egg nearby :D
